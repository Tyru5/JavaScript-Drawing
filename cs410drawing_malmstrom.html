  • The animation is quite interesting, yet awesome. I wanted to make my animation as realistic as possible. Therefore instead of showing the ball being 'sucked' into the massive balck hole, I show the ball gravitating to what is known as the event horizon. This is essentially the point of no return and once an object, in this case it is the red ball, passes the event horizon, nothing, not even light can escape the gravitational pull.
    Therefore, to an observer watching the ball 'fall' into the black hole, one would notice that it would freeze in time. After a couple of minutes, the object would begin to turn red and more red (red shift) until finally the object seems to disappear.
    In my opinion, black holes are horrifyingly interesting and I would highly recommend watching this video to learn and understand more.
